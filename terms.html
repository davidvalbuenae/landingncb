INCORPORATION OF NCB Miles Terms & Conditions: The NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS are subject to the NCB MILES Terms & Conditions as published on www.ncbmiles.com and these NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS Terms & Conditions. ELIGIBLE PURCHASES: The rewards are calculated on eligible non-business purchases, less returns and adjustments for each statement cycle (“Net Purchases”). Non-business purchases are purchases of goods and services for personal, household or family purposes. Business related purchases do not qualify for earning rewards. Cash advances, purchases of money orders, drafts or other cash equivalents, balance transfers from other cards or loans, interest, account payments, fees, service charges and purchase returns do not qualify for NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS.

HOW TO EARN NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS: You will earn one point for each US$ dollar spent or equivalent in Jamaican Dollars on all Net Purchases. You will earn one point for each US$ dollar spent or equivalent in Jamaican Dollars on all Net Purchases at government departments and agencies, up to a maximum of JMD$150,000 per annum. Government departments and agencies include merchants such as Jamaica Customs and the Inland Revenue Department. Government expenditures in excess of $150,000 per annum are classified as business related purchases.

REWARDS: The NCB Mastercard Black reward program allows cardholders to earn 1 point for every $1US or the Jamaican equivalent spent on eligible purchases. Points are accumulated and applied to the customers reward account after each statement is generated. Cardholders can self-redeem points for special offers extended by Visa including travel, hotel accommodation, car rental and cruises by downloading the NCB MILES mobile app or visiting www.ncbmiles.com. Kindly note that the calculation, award and redemption of UpperMiles are the sole responsibility of Novae and that NCB bears no liability in relation to same.

ADDITIONAL DETAILS ON NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS CALCULATION: Rewards will not be awarded to cardholder accounts that are not in good standing or suspended on the statement date. All rewards accrued for the statement cycle up to that date will be forfeited. If an NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARD account is closed at any time prior to the statement date, for any reason, all rewards accrued up to that time will be forfeited.NCB MASTERCARD BLACK REWARDS are based on the Jamaican dollar amount of Net Purchases- you will receive one point for each US$ dollar spent or equivalent in Jamaican Dollars .A fraction of a cent will be rounded up to the nearest cent. NCB may choose not to credit rewards on Net Purchases, which on a monthly basis exceeds your credit limit. Unless forfeited as described herein, reward points shall be valid only for four (4) years from the date of accumulation (the “Validity Period”). NCB reserves the right to adjust the Validity Period at any time within its sole discretion.

DISCREPANCIES: Cardholders must advise NCB of any discrepancies in Net Purchases no later than 25 days after the statement print date. Otherwise, the Net Purchases amount (except for erroneous adjustments) will be deemed to be correct.
